Welcome to NINEC Agriculture

About Our Company

NINEC Agriculture offers contracting and consulting services in production of tobacco, wheat, cotton, soyabean castor bean, and horticultural crops. We monitor contracted crops for the full cycle from land preparation to delivery to the contracting offtaker. With our experience we can offer and manage seed multiplication programs for a wide range of crops. We offer consulting services to farmers and organizations with interests in agriculture. We offer extension and training services for farmers and other agriculture value chain players. On farm field demonstrations, farmer field schools and field days are part of what is on offer on imparting technical knowledge to farmers. Individual farmer monitoring and mentoring is also provided.

Vision

To be the largest agricultural contracting and consulting organization in Africa.

Mission

To offer winning contracting and consulting agricultural solutions.

Values

Excellence, Ethical, Hard Work, Innovation, Sustainability.
